Women in Sport

Here are a handul of many inspiring women in Sport!

Allyson Felix

Allyson Felix beat Usain Bolt's record of 'holding the most gold medals at the track and field World Championships', just 10 months after having a baby! She won her 12th gold medal running in the 4x400m relay team in September 2019.

Here are some more...

Serena Williams

Serena Williams is a tennis champion! Has won 23 Grand Slam titles in her life - which is more than any man or woman in the open era! She has also won 14 Grand slam doubles titles, along with her tennis playing sister - Venus.

Rebecca Adlington

Rebeccas Adlington first got into swimming at the age of 3, and fell in love with it!From then she went on to win two gold medals in the Beijing Olympics in 2008, and two bronze medals in the London Olympics in 2012!

Alex Morgan

Alex Morgan is an football player from America. She plays as a striker and is also captain for the Orlando Pride team.

Simone Biles

Simone Biles is an American gymnast who was part of the "Famous Five" US team who won gold medals at the 2012 Olympics in 2016.
