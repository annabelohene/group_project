Women in STEM

Let's meet some inspiring women in Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics!

Katherine Johnson

Katherine was one of the first African-American women to work as a NASA scientist. The work she did was essential for the success of the first U.S. crewed spaceflights!

Meet the inspirational women in STEM

Alice Bentinck
Cofounder of CodeFirst:Girls, a nonprofit organisation providing free web programming courses for female university students.
Joan Clarke
Worked as a code-breaker at Bletchley Park during WWII.
Lauren Esposito
Cofounder of the network 500 Queer Scientists which aims to highlight incredible individuals and help form connections amongst LGBTQ+ STEM workers.
Aoibhinn Ni Shuilleabhain
Mathematics lecturer and advocate for women in mathematics.
