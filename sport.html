Women in Sport

Let's meet some inspiring women in Sport!

Allyson Felix

Allyson competes in the 100, 200 and 400 metre races in athletics. She also competes in relay races. She has won 12th gold medals! This is the record for the most gold medals at the track and field World Championships - more than Usain Bolt!

Meet the inspirational women in Sport

Rebecca Adlington
Adlington set up her own 'learn to swim' program, which has a mission to see every child in the UK leave primary school being able to swim 25 metres!
Helen Glover
Ranked the number 1 female rower in the world since 2015!
Abby Wambach
A female footballer who is now an activist for female empowerment!
Nadia Comaneci
The first woman to score a perfect 10 in gymnastics!
